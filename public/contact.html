Who's behind this thing?

 

We are a small camp of friends who have been coming to the playa for over 12 years.

 

We've taken breaks here and there. Sometimes missing years to grow up and figure out life.

 

Over the years we've brought you Camp Lite Brite and The Black Rock Brewing Company.

 

Some of us have even been married on the playa.
