What to put in your Porta Potty

 

Bring whatever the hell you want to bring. There is nothing set in stone. Here are some of the things we recommend to get you started.

 

Air Freshener

Ok this one is set in stone. The single biggest thing you can do is make your bathroom smell nice.

 

Toliet Seat Covers

 

Lysol type bathroom cleaning spray

People can spray the seat and wipe before sitting down.

 

Hand and body lotions

 

Spray deodorant

 

Lighting

Battery powered or glow stick.

 

Perfume and cologne

Go buy the cheep imitation kind from Longs. Imagine smelling like Calvin Klein when exiting.

 

3M Removable Hooks

So people can hang thier purses and dinner jackets.

 

Photos or images for the walls.

Just make sure they are water proofed with packaging tape.
