How it works

It's so simple even a caveman can do it.

 

 

Before the event

1. You pledge to bring a basket of items to gift to the porta potties.

See the Items page for ideas

 

2. Go buy things you'd want in a bathroom.

Be creative and inspired.

 

3. Download the Adopt-A-Porta Potty Template.

Put your name or your camp name on it.

 

 

During the event

1. When you are on playa go pick a porta potty near your camp. Secure your basket inside.

At the end of the week go retrieve your basket.

 

2. Tape your adoption sign on the outside.

 

3. Honor, and defend the porta potty with your life.

We recommend posting a 24 hr sentry with a sharp weapon. If people disrespect your bathroom, remove pieces of their body accordingly.
