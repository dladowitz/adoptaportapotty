Leave No Trace

 

The guys cleaning the porta potties have a very tough job as it is. We are dedicated to making sure this project does't make thier job worse. Or they'll put and end to it.

 

So here are some guidelines:

 

Make sure everything is secured down with a zip ties, duct tape and extra strong nylon sting .

This is so nothing falls into the toliet or is thrown in by drunk fools. This is  Burning Man's main concern. There is a big fear that  people will toss plastic bottles into the toliets. If this happens they will shut down our project and remove the items. So we HAVE to address these concerns before hand.

Soon we will have photo suggestions on how to do this. It needs to be "drunken jerk" proofed. Nothing 100lb nylon string and 8 wraps of duct tape can't handle.

 

Ensure everything is waterproof.

The bathrooms are sprayed down a few times a day. You can put things in large zip lock bags (like toliet seat covers). You can also seal things with packaging tape (like photos or drawings)

 

Come back and remove your items at the end of the event.

I mean why wouldn't you want to take home lotion 500 other people have touched?
